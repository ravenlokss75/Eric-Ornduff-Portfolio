Cavity Filling

Rust Removal

Neutralizing rust on your car basically means removing corrosion from the car. An abrasive agent, such as an abrasive wheel or sandpaper of different grits, is usually used to cut through the surface of the corroding area and car metal till a clear metal surface is visible.
